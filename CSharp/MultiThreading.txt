Multitasking: is a concept to running multiple processes/tasks parallelly.
Multithreading: is a concept of running multiple threads within single process parallelly.

Multitasking is like your computer running multiple apps at once.
Multithreading is like one app doing multiple things at once.


Process: is a running application in Operating system.
Thread: A light weight unit of process.

A process has at least one thread which is commonly called as main thread.

A Single process can have multiple threads.
